Where can you find us ?

Le Lotus Bungalow in Foulpointe is a serene hotel complex located just 1.3 km from the beautiful Foulpointe beach. The location is conveniently accessible via RN5 and is surrounded by lush landscapes and a variety of local attractions. Guests can enjoy luxurious amenities including a pristine swimming pool, a gourmet restaurant, and a relaxing spa. Ideal for both relaxation and adventure, Le Lotus Bungalow provides a peaceful retreat with easy access to beach activities and local cultural experiences.
